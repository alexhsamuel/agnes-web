Who We Are

Our mission is to inspire people to give plants to their friends, family, coworkers, and crushes by offering resilient plants in good-looking pots with customizable elements and easy delivery.

Give People Plants

We believe that plants make good gifts because they provide us with the greenery we crave in our indoor lives, they have positive effects on our environments and well-being, and they keep growing with proper care.

Drop 01 / Sansevieria

For our pilot run, we’ve chosen Sansevieria (săn′sə-vîr′ē-ə), an excellent plant for busy New Yorkers since it can handle low to bright light, requires minimal water, and filters nasty stuff out of the air.
